About My Travel Map

My Travel Map is a personal, visual journal designed to document your journeys, track visited countries, and archive beautiful memories across the globe.

How to Use

Interactive Maps

Navigate between Map 1 (standard view) and Map 2 (clustered marker view) using the navigation menu. Click on any location marker to open the photo strip preview. Click any photo to see it in full resolution gallery view.

Travel Photo Books

Access your curated photobooks via the top menu. Book 1 groups your trips by countries and cities, while Book 2 presents an elegant collapsible timeline of your travels over the years.

Add New Places

Use the Add page to search for places, select coordinates directly by clicking on the interactive mini-map, upload multiple photos, and persist them locally in your browser's memory.

Tech Stack

Built with modern web technologies: Flask back-end, Folium & Leaflet.js maps, and PWA caching service workers for full offline availability.
